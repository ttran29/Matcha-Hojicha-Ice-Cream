Matcha & Hojicha Ice Cream

Delicious homemade recipes, no machines required!

Matcha Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons matcha powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup lactose-free milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks and condensed milk until smooth.
  2. In another bowl, mix matcha powder with a small amount of warm lactose-free milk until it forms a paste.
  3. Gradually add the rest of the lactose-free milk to the matcha paste, whisking to combine.
  4. Combine the matcha mixture with the egg yolk mixture.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the heavy cream until it thickens slightly (soft peaks).
  6. Fold the heavy cream into the matcha mixture gently.
  7. Pour into a shallow container and freeze for 6-8 hours, stirring every hour to prevent ice crystals.
